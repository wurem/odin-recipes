Lasagna

Sunday, family lunch: if we try to close our eyes, she comes to mind, the queen of the italian holiday table: lasagna.


First of all, what is lasagna? It is nothing more than a series of rectangular sheets of pasta, made from egg or durum wheat, boiled, then arranged in layers in a baking dish or oven dish, alternating with a filling, which can vary according to regional customs.
The traditional recipe calls for meat sauce and bechamel sauce.
